= DLP notes

https://www.documentliberation.org/projects/

- auto-rewrite:

----
clang-tidy -header-filter=^$PWD -export-fixes=clang/foo.yaml $(git ls-files|egrep '(cxx|cpp)$')
clang-apply-replacements -remove-change-desc-files clang
----

- silly names after loop-convert:

----
git show|grep 'for.*m_.* :'
----

- .clang-tidy template:

----
Checks: '-*,bugprone-too-small-loop-variable'
WarningsAsErrors: '*'

----
